# Transpose v0

Implement matrix transposition using shared memory:

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
4 & 5 & 6
\end{pmatrix}^{T}=
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 4 \\
2 & 5 \\
3 & 6
\end{pmatrix}
$$

All matrices use row-major layout.
This version has no benchmarks, but later versions will include them.
